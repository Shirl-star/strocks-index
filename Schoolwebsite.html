Our Mission

St Rocks High School is an open-admission school that prepares all students for college admissions and successful careers. St Rocks High provides a rigorous high school curriculum with an emphasis in science and mathematics in a supportive environment of learning and respect that prepares students to make informed choices about post-secondary pursuits.

New students are welcome to join.
